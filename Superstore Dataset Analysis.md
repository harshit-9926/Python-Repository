# 📊 Superstore Sales Data Analysis (EDA using Python)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Superstore dataset to uncover key insights related to sales, profit,
customer behavior, and regional performance. The objective is to analyze business trends and provide actionable insights that can
help improve profitability and decision-making.

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains retail transaction data with the following key fields:

* **Order Date** – Date of purchase
* **Region / State / City** – Geographic information
* **Category & Sub-Category** – Product classification
* **Ship Mode** – Shipping mode like Standard, First class 
* **Sales** – Revenue generated
* **Profit** – Profit earned per transaction
* **Quantity** – Number of units sold
* **Discount** – Discount applied on products

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

### 🔹 Sales & Profit Analysis

* Analyzed overall sales and profit distribution
* Compared sales vs profit to identify gaps

### 🔹 Category & Sub-Category Analysis

* Evaluated performance of different product categories
* Identified top-selling and low-performing sub-categories

### 🔹 Regional Analysis

* Compared sales and profit across regions, states, and cities
* Identified high-performing and underperforming regions

### 🔹 Time-Based Analysis

* Analyzed monthly and yearly sales trends
* Identified seasonal patterns in sales and profit

### 🔹 Discount Impact Analysis

* Studied the effect of discounts on profit margins
* Identified cases where high discounts lead to losses

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* High sales do not always translate into high profit due to excessive discounts
* Certain categories and sub-categories contribute the majority of revenue
* Some regions generate strong sales but low profit, indicating inefficiencies
* Seasonal trends observed in sales performance across months
* Discount-heavy products often result in negative profit margins

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Optimize discount strategies to prevent profit loss
* Focus on high-margin product categories
* Improve performance in low-profit regions through targeted strategies
* Leverage seasonal trends for marketing and inventory planning
* Reduce dependency on low-performing products
